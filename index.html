About Us

What We Do

Cove.net LLC (Cove) is focused on developing, managing and securing both the High Voltage & Low Voltage Grids. With access to multiple Network & Security Operating Centers (NOC/SOC), Cove has a worldwide reach. From fiber optic backbone design/build to a variety of cyber security audits, we will “Build to Protect”. Now with the addition of “Contact Tracing” and other COVID related technologies, we have taken a very small but technical step into personal security.
