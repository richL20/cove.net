Services and Solutions

TECHNOLOGY

Our Technology Services focus on developing robust, secure and stable technology solutions for business, government and communities. By incorporating common sense solutions into seemingly daunting deliverables, we can ensure at the start of the project that we will safely deliver the product to completion. Our services include:

  • Backbone & Infrastructure Architecture Solutions
  • Rural & Urban Broadband Deployment
  • Collocation Builds and Installation
  • Hospitality and Healthcare WiFi Design and Builds
  • Application & Web, Design & Development
  • “Dark Web” Scanning and Reporting with Ongoing Systems Monitoring
  • Commercial Off the Shelf (COTS) Integration
  • Data Cleansing & Conversion
  • Database Design, Development, & Maintenance
  • Verification & Validation Service
  • Knowledge of Edwards, Siemens, Honeywell and other Alarm & Security Systems

CONSULTING

Our Consulting Services focus on periodical audits of Alarm & Fire Systems, WiFi & IP Bandwidth Surveys and finding the best backbone service providers. We can also assist in commissioning services.

General Areas Include:

  • Change Management & Facilitation Support
  • CMMI Process Development and Assessment Services
  • Security & Network Assessment
  • Security & Network Assessment
  • Training for Networking, Software and CAD
